### Usage Instructions

- Use the save button (blue) on the right to save any tasks or
  meetings. The information will be saved even when you refresh the
  browser. Use the current day colors in order to navigate what day of
  week it current is and what time your weekly meetings or tasks may procede
  throughout your workday.
